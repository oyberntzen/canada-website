Info About the CN Tower

The CN Tower is a world famous and iconic skyscraper in Toronto. The skyscraper is over 550 meters tall and was the worldâ€™s tallest building for 32 years (1975-2007). CN stands for Canadian Nation, which is the company that built the tower. Today, the building is a popular tourist attraction with a restaurant rotating 360 degrees every 72 minutes. One of the special things with the restaurant is that there is a glass floor where you can look down at the ground 342 meters below. Furthermore, you can walk on the top roof of the restaurant, which is 356 meters over the ground. This is a spectacular way to experience the amazing view from the tower.
