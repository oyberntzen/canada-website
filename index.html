Everything You Need to Know About Canada

Introduction

Canada is a beautiful country in North America. Visiting the country is something you should put on your bucket list, and here are some reasons why. First, there are tons of great attractions and activities in Canada that you can experience. Next, the wildlife in Canada is unique, with many interesting animals. Finally, there are many popular sports in Canada, and you should definitely check out a sports event while visiting. Wildlife

The wildlife of Canada is diverse, with over 80 000 recorded species, including both plants and animals. Even though the number of recorded species is big, it is expected that there exist about 80 000 additional unrecorded species. Human activity and global warming are reasons for many species going extinct, and today there are 800 species at risk of being lost. For this reason, many national parks have been developed in Canada to protect the plants and animals. There are restrictions for what you can do in the national parks, but you can still visit them to experience the wildlife. However, it is recommended to learn a bit about the animals before travelling, as some of the animals can be dangerous.

Sports

Sports are a big part of Canadian culture. The most popular sports in Canada are ice hockey, lacrosse, gridiron football, soccer, basketball, curling and baseball. Ice hockey is the official winter sport and is probably the most famous Canadian sport as it started in Canada. Lacrosse is the official summer sport. More details on ice hockey and lacrosse are given in the sections below. In addition, there are many other popular sports in Canada, and tons of famous Canadian sport athletes. Some of the all-time most famous Canadian sport athletes are wrestler Roddy Piper, ice hockey player Wayne Gretzky, wrestler Bret Hart, basketball player Steve Nash, and basketball player Andrew Wiggins. When visiting Canada, you should definitely see a sports event, even if you are not interested in sports.
