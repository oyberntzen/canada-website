Info About the Niagara Falls

The Niagara Falls is one of the worldâ€™s most spectacular waterfalls. The Niagara Falls consists of three waterfalls, named the Horseshoe Falls, the American Falls and the Bridal Veils Falls. The Niagara Falls is located on the border between Canada and the US, with the Horseshoe Falls on the Canadian side, and the American Falls and Bridal Veils Falls on the American side. The Horseshoe Falls is the biggest and most spectacular of the three waterfalls. For this reason, it is the most popular for tourists too. At the most the Niagara Falls is 56 meters tall. Up to 2,6 million liters of water flow pour over the Niagara Falls every second, which is slightly more than the average yearly water consumption of three Canadian people. Just seeing the waterfalls is amazing, but there are other more spectacular ways to experience the Niagara Falls. Some examples are in the next section.

Ways to Experience the Waterfalls

Spectacular ways to experience the Niagara Falls:

  • Get an amazing view of the Niagara Falls from the Niagara Falls Observation Tower.
  • Journey Behind the Falls offers a close-up view of the Horseshoe Falls and viewing portals behind the waterfall.
  • Hornblower Niagara Cruise offers a relaxed boat trip to all the three waterfalls.

Other activities in the area:

  • Skylon Tower is a 235-meter-tall tower and offers a view of the whole city.
  • Niagara Skywheel is the largest observation wheel in Canada.
  • Niagara Speedway is a huge, elevated go-kart course.
  • Niagara-on-the-Lake is a small city with a mid-19th-century architecture style.
